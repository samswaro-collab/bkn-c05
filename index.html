📊 8-Quarter Analysis — GE (NYSE)
GE Aerospace became a standalone pure-play aerospace company in April 2024 after spinning off GE Vernova. Revenue grew +21% in FY2025 to $39.7B with orders +32%. The challenge: a $1B manufacturing modernization commitment, tariff exposure of $500M+, and supply chain bottlenecks limiting engine production. Engineering services demand is at an all-time high.
